Stock Symbol Type Racer

This typing race was developed to aid day traders in becoming quick and efficient at typing up stock symbols that pass through their filters. Symbols are not case sensitive. Hit enter when the symbol is typed. There is no penalty for mistyped words, however they will not count towards your WPM. Your score is calculated as follows:

  • +1 point for a correct word
  • +1 point for each correct word in a row
  • Streak is reset to 0 for each incorrect word
  • At the end of the race, your score is multiplied by your WPM

Happy Racing!
